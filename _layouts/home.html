Our Projects

All our projects are free. If you are a clinician using our resources, we would greatly appreciate a donation (though not required). If you feature our projects, please just link back to us. Thank you!

Non-police Warmlines

A collection of warm, hot, and crisis lines that never call for police or an active rescue.

  • View Warmlines

Mini Case Management Tool

A webapp that features a library of resources located in Multnomah County, OR. The resources are all free or sliding scale and include things like community activities (like art, volunteeting, gardening, etc), in person recovery supports, well being supports (such a SNAP, support for professional clothing, etc), and online peer support and recovery groups. All in person supports include bus routes.

  • View Tool

The Big Virtual Peer Calendar

A Google calendar that includes free, online peer support groups from 7 different nonclinical, anticarceral peer support organizations in one place.

  • View Calendar

Safety Plan Zine

A free workbook to use to use as a crisis tool kit. Workbook features brightly colored flowers, jelly beans, and sprinkles and uses nonclinical, friendly language.

  • View Safety Plan Zine
